Death by tree.

Main Section

Coming to the conclusion that wandering through the forest looking for shelter is
a better idea than stumbly around hoplessly through the forest for the door you came from,
you strike out into the jungle. As the sky grows darker with each minute you begin to
loose hope and soon you find yourself in pitch black darkness. Suddenly a lightning
strikes a massive tree beside you and cracks it in half. You don't scream or run
as the tree falls and crushes you. You die.
