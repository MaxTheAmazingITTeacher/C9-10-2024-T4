Run de popcorn.

Main Section

Thinking quickly you pull out your emergency popcorn out and throw it at the creature before you bolt,
leaving it behind. You run terrified through the forest along the path until you reach the door. You
throw it open and charge into the S-Block hall, then you blindly run into the exit, breaking the
locked door and probably getting an injury. Permanently traumatised you go down to B11.
