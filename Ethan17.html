The desert.

Main Section

You open the door and imediately a strong wind blows dust into the face. Coughing you blink
your eyes back open and squint from the bright light. Towering sand dunes stretch as far as
the eye can see, but as you look around you see a faint dark shape off in the distance.
Maybe you should check it out, though it seems awfully far away.
