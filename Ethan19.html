The oasis.

Main Section

You start to walk towards the oasis, but soon thirst begins to take a toll on you and you are soon
stumbling through the desert. However there was no real chance of getting to it as in the dip
between two dunes you collapse from exhaustion and thirst. As you lie there in the sand with
the sun burning your skin you regret going out into the desert. You die.
